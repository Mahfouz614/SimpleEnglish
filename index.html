📝 Easy Grammar Steps

1 Don't mix up: Your vs. You're

Your belongs to you ➡️ "Is this your book?"

You're is short for 'You are' ➡️ "Hey, you're doing a great job!"

2 Speaking about the Past (-ed)

To talk about yesterday, usually just add -ed to the action word:

  • Today I walk ➡️ Yesterday I walked
  • Today I cook ➡️ Yesterday I cooked
